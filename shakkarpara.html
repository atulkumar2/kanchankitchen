Description

Shakkarpara are sweet, crispy diamond-shaped pastries made from refined flour (maida) dough enriched with ghee, fried until golden, and then coated in a thick sugar syrup that crystallises into a beautifully crunchy glaze. Mildly flavoured with cardamom, they have a delicate sweetness and satisfying crunch. A popular Diwali and festive sweet that stores well for weeks at room temperature.
