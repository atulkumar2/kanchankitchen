Description

Namak Para are classic Indian savoury crackers shaped into small diamonds or strips. Made from a dough of whole wheat or refined flour, seasoned with carom seeds (ajwain), cumin, salt, and a touch of ghee for richness, then deep-fried to a light golden finish. They are crunchy, mildly spiced, and pair beautifully with chai, chutneys, or enjoyed on their own. A festive staple, especially during Diwali.
