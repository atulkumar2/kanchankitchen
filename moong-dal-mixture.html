Description

Moong Dal Mixture is a crispy, light snack made from split and skinned green gram (moong dal) that is soaked, dried, and deep-fried until perfectly golden. The fried dal is then tossed with rock salt, black pepper, turmeric, and a hint of chaat masala. Occasionally blended with sev and puffed rice for added texture, it makes for a wholesome, addictive, tea-time snack loved across India.
